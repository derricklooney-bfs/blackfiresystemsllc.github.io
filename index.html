Support Model

Proactive monitoring, scheduled support, and clear scope

Blackfire managed service plans are not sold as unlimited 24/7 help desk access. The model is built around monitoring, preventative maintenance, scheduled administration, and support as needed for covered services.

Included support focuses on

  • Continuous platform monitoring and alert review.
  • Security event response based on plan coverage.
  • Routine patching, maintenance, and administration.
  • Ticket-based remote support for covered services.
  • Scheduled changes such as onboarding, offboarding, account updates, and configuration changes.

Quoted separately when needed

  • After-hours support and emergency support outside the selected plan.
  • Major upgrades, migrations, new deployments, and one-time projects.
  • On-site service, cabling, hardware installation, and physical remediation work.
  • Out-of-scope support requests or excessive ad hoc support needs.
  • Incident response work beyond normal monitoring and triage.
